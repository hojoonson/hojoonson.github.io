I received my M.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Yonsei University at Seoul, Republic of Korea in 2020. I also received a B.S. degree, double major in Mechanical Engineering and Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Yonsei University at Seoul, Republic of Korea in 2017.
My Master's thesis topic is "Automated swept path analysis of oversized cargo transportation through deep reinforcement learning". I am currently a member of NEXTLab Co., Ltd. as a Senior Research Engineer. I am also a member of Mensa Korea.

My research focuses on various applications of Knowledge Based Systems to retrieve key information from system and support human decision-making. Specifically, my research interests include Machine Learning, Multi-Agent Systems, and Decision Support Systems.
In addition, I am also interested in creative approaches of Human-Computer Interaction with eXtended Reality. To be specific, it includes User-Centered Design, Tangible User Interface, and Context Awareness.
